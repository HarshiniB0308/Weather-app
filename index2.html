Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service.

Is AeroWeather completely free?

Yes! AeroWeather utilizes free open-source APIs like Open-Meteo to provide you with world-class data without any subscription costs.

Are the forecasts real-time?

Absolutely. We fetch live data requests every time you search, ensuring you receive the latest updates direct from meteorological models.

How does the location feature work?

By using the HTML5 Geolocation API, we request your browser's permission to find your coordinates, which are securely used to fetch your local weather. We don't store your location.
